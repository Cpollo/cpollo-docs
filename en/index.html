Description

CPOLLO is a platform which is defined as an open and a crowdsourced project which utilizes the power of blockchain technology and decentralization to promote faster, more secure, and more efficient crowdsourcing services. The CPOLLO platform aims to serve as a transparent and unbiased hub for Projects.
